WHO ARE WE?

Robomix is a small programming studio located in Ukraine, consisting of Andrew, Dima, Max and Dan. All four of us are graduates from the Technical University, where we learned about each others unique work methods and saw the potential to do great stuff together.

Established in the summer of 2008, Robomix is a competent and curious team specialised in both web and mobile applications. In order to truly harness our expertise, our main focus area is in the intersection between the frontend and the backend spheres.

WHAT WE DOING?

Developing and support of the new and existing websites, server's side, client-server Android and iOS-applications for customers in the USA, United Kingdom, Germany, Australia and more countries with the usage of different programming languages like PHP, Java, JavaScript, TypeScript, Kotlin, Python etc.
